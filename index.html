Introduction

If you are looking for a hotel in Ichinoseki, the entrance to Iwate-ji,

full of poetic sentiments, then make a reservation at Hotel Matsu no Ka Ichinoseki.

A one-minute walk from Ichinoseki Station.

A convenient base for sightseeing and business trips to Hiraizumi and Motsu-ji Temple.


From breakfasts to enjoy the local dish, "Hittsumi" to boxed lunches,

meals can be prepared to suit your style and budget.

About Shogetsu-do

We have been working together with Ichinoseki Station for more than 130 years since its opening.

Marking history together with the development of the city.

Today, thanks to the support of our many customers, we offer several types of lunch boxes at our store, in the station, and on board the trains.

Ever since our establishment, we have been aiming to create “Eki-ben lunch boxes that are close to the hearts of travelers.”
